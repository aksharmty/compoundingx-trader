About Compounding in Spot Trading

Compounding in spot trading means reusing your profits in the next trade to grow your capital faster. With every successful cycle, your capital increases and so does your next trade size. Over time, this creates exponential growth.

How this Calculator Works

You enter your starting capital, target capital, first buy amount, buy increment %, take profit %, and fee. The calculator then estimates how many trading cycles are required to reach your target by compounding your profits.

Why Use Compounding?

  • Small capital can grow into a large amount with patience.
  • Perfect for long-term traders who reinvest profits.
  • Shows the real power of consistent profit-taking.
