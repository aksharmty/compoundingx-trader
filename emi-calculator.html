What is an EMI?

EMI ka full form hai Equated Monthly Installment. Ye ek fixed monthly payment hota hai jo aapko apne loan ke tenure ke dauran har mahine bank ya financial institution ko dena padta hai. EMI me do parts hote hain – ek hissa principal (loan ka asli amount) aur doosra hissa interest (byaj).

EMI Formula

EMI calculate karne ka formula hai: EMI = P × r × (1+r)n / ((1+r)n − 1) Yahan P = Principal (Loan Amount), r = Monthly Interest Rate, aur n = Tenure in months. Example: Agar aap ₹5,00,000 ka loan lete hain 5 saal (60 months) ke liye 10% annual interest par, to EMI calculator turant aapko batayega ki aapko kitna mahine bharna hoga.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Is EMI fixed every month?
A. Haan, fixed-rate loan me EMI same rehti hai poore tenure me. Floating-rate loans me change ho sakti hai.

Q. Can I prepay my loan?
A. Haan, agar aap extra payment karte hain to aapka outstanding balance kam ho jaata hai aur interest bhi bachta hai.

Q. Which loans can I calculate?
A. Ye calculator personal, home, car, education, aur business loans ke liye kaam karta hai.
